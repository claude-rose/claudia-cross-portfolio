About

Claudia is a budding front-end developer from the Gold Coast, Australia. Loving all things science and technology, Claudia is studying a double degree in Science and Mathematics, majoring in Physics and Applied and Computational Mathematics at the Queensland University of Technology. Claudia also sits on the executive committee of the QUT Women in Mathematics club, supporting and engaging with other women and gender diverse STEM students. Craving further study, she took on the SheCodes coding workshops where she is continuing to persue a new found passion for web development.
